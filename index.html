School Science Fair

Is done every year

Science fair project display A science fair is a competitive event, hosted by our school. The distinguishing characteristic of a science fair is that project entries employ the scientific method to test a hypothesis. Science fairs are not exhibits or mere displays of projects.



school library

A quiet space for all ages

A library is a collection of materials, books or media that are accessible for use and not just for display purposes. A library provides physical (hard copies) or digital access (soft copies) materials, and may be a physical location or a virtual space, or both.



school auditorium

Beautiful performances

The School is supported by a fully air conditioned School Auditorium which is equipped with the most modern and audio visual facilities and a massive seating capacity of approximately 800 people. The sound proof auditorium hosts numerous events including Annual day, Award functions , Inter School Competitions and Zonal level competitions.
